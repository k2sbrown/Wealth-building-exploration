Appreciating assets = ownership of any of: retirement accounts (401k/thrift, IRA), business equity, future pension, securities (mutual funds, savings bonds, stocks, bonds), other real property, other financial assets, cash-value life insurance, or primary residence (9 categories total).

HH without appreciating assets: percent shown is that group's share of its own household universe. % home is only appreciating asset: share of all households (incl. non-owners) whose sole appreciating holding is a primary residence — a measure of wealth concentration risk. Mean # of types counts distinct categories held (unconditional means: White 2.49, Black 1.54, Hispanic 1.26, Other 2.09, All 2.22).
